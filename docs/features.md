# Features of Recipe Book

* **Atomic Ingredient scaling**
    - Allows users to scale recipes up or down without worrying about losing flavor ratios
* **Meal planning**
    - Allows users to plan out meals for the week, including generating grocery lists.
* **Recipe filtering**
    - Allows users to filter recipes by ingredient, cooking time, and more
* **Recipe organization**
    - Allows users to organize if, categorize, and tag recipes for easy retrieval
* **Recipe notes**
    - Allows users to add notes to recipes, including tips, substitutions, and variations
* **Recipe rating**
    - Allows users to rate recipes and keep track of their favorites
* **Recipe sharing**
    - Allows users to share recipes with friends and family
* **Recipe discovery**
    - Allows users to discover new recipes based on their preferences and cooking history
